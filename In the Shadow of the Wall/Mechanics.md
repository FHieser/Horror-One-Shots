# Mechanics


## Wall Breakdown
- 27 Days until the wall breaks from arrival
  - Timeline Schedule

## Stress

- Max 12
  - Should have impact on Gameplay
- Stress Causes
  - Should generate 1-3
  - Causes
    - Long Drive
    - Mysteries
    - Not eating
    - Anomalies
    - Accidents
    - Players can give themselves Stress
- Stress Relief
  - Should reduce by 1-3
  - Options
    - Walking
    - Solving Mysteries
    - Talking Relaxing
    - Player Choices on Player Genration

## Player Genration

- What are you fleeing from that caused your Burnout?
  - Work (Selfimposed/Exterior)
  - Relation (Selfimposed/Exterior)
  - Health (Selfimposed/Exterior)

- What relaxes you?
  - Examples
    - Being alone / Being Social
    - Sport / Creating
    - Reading / Driving
