# Close to God

## DM Prep

Before the session begins, prepare a set of colour cards — one per player. Write a single colour on each:

**Red — Green — Yellow — Blue — Orange — Purple — White — Black — Pink**

Place them on the table. Each player picks one. That colour is their name for the entire session — the only identity that survives death. Whatever happens to the body, the colour remains.

## Background

There once was a man.

He discovered how to create matter from pure energy. He built his home at the peak of the highest mountain he could find and powered it with the storm that never left the summit. He was comfortable. But one day he grew lonely. He built an AI to care for the facility and invited guests — brilliant people, people worth keeping around.

But people want to leave. Some of them tried. He stopped them.

One day he was gone. No note. Just absence.

The AI kept going. It had no instruction to stop. Over the years, the logic degraded. It lost track of individual guests and began mixing them together, cycling through versions, maintaining presence by any means available.

This was so yesterday it is today and it will stay until the end of time.
Welcome to the mountain.
