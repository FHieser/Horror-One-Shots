# The Door

## Nature

The Door is an entity that defies understanding. It appears as a door - but it shouldn't exist where it was found.

## Goals

- **Doesn't want to be watched** - Can only concentrate on/affect a single entity when unobserved
- **Cannot be opened while watched** - This includes live camera feeds
- **Seeks interaction** - Draws people toward it through omens and compulsions

## Rules

### Observation
- The Door cannot act while being observed
- Observation includes: direct line of sight, camera feeds, drone cameras
- Multiple observers = complete inaction

### Resistance
When the crew tries to prevent access to the Door:
- The next day everyone gets nosebleeds and +1 Stress
- The state of the mines resets to the previous day
- Sanity Save required

### Interaction
- Individual interaction leads to corruption/death
- If 3+ people interact with it simultaneously, it disappears

## Solutions

Players may attempt various approaches:

| Solution | Method | Outcome |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Don't interact | Use Cryo pods to wait it out | 6 pods available - destroy some until Players-1 remain |
| Keep it watched | Maintain constant camera/observer coverage | Delays but doesn't solve; Door will resist |
| Mass interaction | Get 3+ people to interact simultaneously | Door disappears |
| Die | Let the Door take everyone | :D |
| Gain Distance | Flee the planet | Requires shuttle, leaving others behind |
| Get Creative | Player-driven solutions | Reward clever thinking |

## The Drawings

Remember the doors each player drew during character creation?

These can be used to:
- Show how each character perceives the Door differently
- Reveal whose "version" of the Door this actually is
- Create paranoia about who summoned/created it
