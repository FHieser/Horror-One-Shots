# Introduction

## Setting

**Planet:** 4d756e726f69 (designation: 4d7)
**Resource:** Titanium
**Extraction Rights:** Daedalus Group

### Mission Goals
- Extract Titanium
- Probe the planet for Terraforming viability

### Isolation Parameters
- **Crew Size:** 7 (Androids included)
- **Next Crew Swap:** 8 months
- **Next Supply Drop:** 2 months
- **Communication Delay:** 4 hours to nearest station

### Company Provisions
- Monthly salary
- Crew attire
- Food and accommodations
- Pagers for everyone

### Starting Equipment
- 1.5 kcr (thousand credits)

## Character Creation

Players answer the following:

1. **What job do they take?** (See crew-roles.csv)
2. **What do they do in their free time?**
3. **How do you really relax?**
4. **Why do they do this job?**
5. **What is their dream?**
6. **Draw the GM a door** *(Important: save these drawings for later)*

## Opening

*Read to players after character creation:*

The crew has been on 4d7 for [X months]. Routine has set in. The mining operations proceed as expected. The terraforming probes return unremarkable data. Communication with the company is sparse but regular.

Today starts like any other day. Until Drone Control's pager goes off in the middle of the night cycle...
