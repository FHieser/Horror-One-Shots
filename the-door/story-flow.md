# Story Flow

## Act 1: The Door

**Trigger:** Drone Control gets woken up by message on their Pager

**Events:**
- One of the Mining Robots found something unusual
- A Door
- Shown on the Monitors

**GM Notes:**
- Let players react naturally
- Establish the impossibility - there shouldn't be a door here
- The Door doesn't do anything yet

---

## Act 2: Investigations

**Player Actions:**
- Let the crew do their investigation
- The Door doesn't react in any way
- The captain will insist on monitoring
- Don't let the drones interact with it

**GM Notes:**
- Build tension through the Door's passivity
- It's more unsettling that it does nothing
- Begin dropping Minor Omens for each character

---

## Act 3: Missing Captain

**Events:**
- The captain is missing
- The feed for the cameras is off
- There is a key item of the Captain laying at the door

**GM Notes:**
- This is the point of no return
- Players must decide how to proceed
- Captain's codes are needed for reactor/life support

---

## Act 4: Omens

**Escalation:**
- Let the players to their devices
- Let the omens loose on the players
- It's just a question of time for the players to go ham on each other

**GM Notes:**
- Transition from Minor to Major Omens
- Each character experiences personalized horror
- Paranoia should be at maximum
- Players may start suspecting each other

---

## Act 5: Chaos

**Resolution:**
- Players will fight themselves about what to do

**Options they may consider:**
- Destroying the door?
- Leaving it alone?
- Going really crazy? (Stress stacks up really hard here)
- Opening it?
- Let it play out
- Find an appropriate ending point

**GM Notes:**
- There is no "correct" ending
- Let the players drive the conclusion
- See entity-the-door.md for possible solutions
